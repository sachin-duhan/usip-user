About USIP

Delhi Technological University proposes to launch University Student Internship Program (USIP) to engage DTU students in various activities of the university. The impetus of the program is to provide opportunity to expose students to real life environment and to help university in improving processes and outcome of the activities undertaken time to time. The student interns are required to work for eight hours a week including weekends and holidays, and will be paid Rs. 2500/- per month.
